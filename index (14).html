Yellowstone黄石公园

est. 1872 · world's first national park

The world's first national park, sitting on an active supervolcano — which is why it holds about half the planet's geysers and over 10,000 hot springs. Old Faithful, Grand Prismatic, bison, and wolves all live here. Our route hits the highlighted areas below.世界上第一个国家公园，坐落在活跃的超级火山之上 — 因此拥有全球约一半的间歇泉和一万多处温泉。老忠实、大棱镜、野牛和狼都在这里。我们的路线会经过下方高亮的区域。

Grand Teton大提顿

est. 1929 · youngest range in the Rockies

The youngest range in the Rockies — peaks rise abruptly ~7,000 ft straight off the valley with no foothills. We're staying on the Idaho (Driggs) side of the range, but the park itself is an easy add-on if anyone wants the Jenny Lake classics. Grand Teton tops out at 13,775 ft.落基山脉中最年轻的山脉 — 山峰从谷底陡然拔起约 2100 米，没有山麓。我们住在山脉的爱达荷（Driggs）一侧，但如果有人想走 Jenny Lake 的经典路线，进公园也很方便。大提顿峰高 13,775 英尺。

Things to Watch For

Danger List危险清单

Yellowstone is wild country, not a theme park. None of this is scary if you respect it — but read it once before you go.黄石是真正的荒野，不是主题公园。只要尊重它，这些都不可怕 — 但出发前请看一遍。

1 Bears熊

Grizzly and black bears are everywhere. Carry bear spray on your hip (not in your pack), make noise on trails, hike in groups, never run, and stay 100 yards away. Store all food in the bear box.灰熊和黑熊到处都有。防熊喷雾挂腰上（别放包里），步道上多出声，结伴而行，绝不要跑，保持 100 码（91米）距离。所有食物放进熊柜。

2 Bison野牛

They look calm but injure more people than bears. They run 30+ mph. Stay at least 25 yards away — more in summer rutting season. Never get between a bison and its path.它们看着温顺，但伤的人比熊还多。能跑超过每小时 30 英里。至少保持 25 码（23米），夏季发情期要更远。绝不要挡在野牛和它要走的路之间。

3 Thermal ground地热区

Stay on the boardwalks — always. The crust is thin and the water below is boiling and acidic. People have died stepping off-trail. Same goes for kids and pets.永远走在栈道上。地壳很薄，下面的水滚烫且具腐蚀性。有人因离开步道而丧命。小孩和宠物同样。

4 Altitude & sun高海拔 & 日晒

Camps sit at 6,800–8,000 ft. You'll dehydrate and tire faster, and the sun is stronger. Drink more water than feels necessary, and wear sunscreen even when it's cool.营地海拔 6,800–8,000 英尺。更容易脱水和疲劳，阳光也更强。多喝水（比你觉得需要的更多），凉快时也要涂防晒。

5 Weather & lightning天气 & 雷电

Storms blow up fast. Nights drop into the 30s–40s°F even in July, and exposed spots like Storm Point draw lightning. Pack warm layers and get off open points if a storm rolls in.暴风雨说来就来。七月夜里也会降到 0–7°C，Storm Point 这样的开阔处易招雷电。带足保暖衣物，雷雨来时迅速离开开阔岬角。

6 Cold, swift water冰冷急流

Rivers and the lake are cold and fast year-round. Don't swim or wade across currents — even strong swimmers get into trouble in minutes.河流和湖水全年又冷又急。不要游泳或涉水横渡 — 再好的泳者几分钟内也可能出事。

7 No signal没信号

Cell service dies inside the park. Download offline maps before you enter, tell someone your plan for each hike, and don't rely on your phone for navigation.园区内手机没信号。进园前下载离线地图，每次徒步前告诉别人你的计划，别指望手机导航。

8 Distance rule距离规则

Park rule: stay 100 yards (91 m) from bears and wolves, and 25 yards (23 m) from everything else. No selfies with wildlife. No feeding anything, including the cute marmots.公园规定：离熊和狼 100 码（91米），离其它动物 25 码（23米）。不要和野生动物自拍，不要喂任何动物，包括可爱的旱獭。
